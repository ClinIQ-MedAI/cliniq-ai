# Wrist and Bone Fracture — Patient Guidance / إرشادات كسور المعصم والعظام

## What a fracture means
A fracture is a break in a bone. On a wrist X-ray the AI may flag a **fracture**,
**surgical metal** (a plate or screw from a previous operation), or a
**periosteal reaction** (a sign the bone is healing or reacting to a process).
Pediatric wrist fractures are among the most common childhood injuries, often
from a fall onto an outstretched hand.

## Common signs
Pain that worsens with movement, swelling, bruising, tenderness over the bone,
reduced grip strength, or a visible deformity. Not every fracture is obvious;
some hairline fractures are only visible on imaging.

## What to do
- Immobilize the area and avoid using it.
- Apply a cold pack (wrapped, not directly on skin) for swelling.
- Seek an orthopedic evaluation — a confirmed fracture usually needs a splint or
  cast, and displaced fractures may need reduction.
- Follow-up imaging checks that the bone is healing in good alignment.

## Red flags — seek urgent care
Numbness or tingling below the injury, a cold or pale hand, an open wound over
the bone, or severe deformity. These can signal nerve or blood-vessel
involvement and need immediate attention.

## بالعربية (ملخص)
الكسر هو انكسار في العظم. أشعة المعصم قد تُظهر كسرًا، أو معدنًا جراحيًا من عملية
سابقة، أو تفاعل السمحاق (علامة على التئام العظم). العلامات الشائعة: ألم يزداد مع
الحركة، تورّم، كدمات. الأهم: ثبّت المكان، ضع كمادة باردة، واستشر أخصائي عظام.
اطلب رعاية عاجلة فورًا عند وجود تنميل، أو برودة/شحوب في اليد، أو جرح مفتوح فوق
العظم، أو تشوّه شديد.

> This is general education, not a diagnosis. A qualified clinician must review
> any imaging finding.
