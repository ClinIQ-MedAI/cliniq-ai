# Prescription & Medication Safety — Patient Guidance / سلامة الأدوية والروشتات

## Reading your prescription
The AI prescription parser extracts the drug name, strength, and dosage from a
handwritten or printed prescription to make it readable. Always confirm the
result against your doctor's or pharmacist's instructions — parsing assists, it
does not replace professional verification.

## Taking medicines safely
- **Follow the dose and schedule exactly.** Do not double a dose if you miss one
  unless told to.
- **Finish antibiotics** for the full course even if you feel better, to avoid
  resistance.
- **Do not share** prescription medicines with others.
- **Check for interactions** — tell your doctor about every medicine, supplement,
  and herbal product you take.
- **Watch expiry dates** and store medicines as directed (some need refrigeration,
  most need a cool, dry place away from children).

## Common issues to report
Rash, swelling of the face or throat, difficulty breathing (possible allergy —
stop and seek urgent care), severe stomach upset, or symptoms that do not
improve. Report side effects to your doctor.

## Antibiotic stewardship
Antibiotics treat bacterial infections, not viral ones like the common cold.
Taking them unnecessarily drives resistance. Only take antibiotics prescribed
for you, for the condition they were prescribed for.

## بالعربية (ملخص)
محلّل الروشتة يستخرج اسم الدواء والجرعة لجعل الروشتة مقروءة، لكن لا يغني عن تأكيد
الطبيب أو الصيدلي. التزم بالجرعة والمواعيد بدقّة، وأكمل كورس المضاد الحيوي كاملًا،
ولا تشارك أدويتك مع غيرك، وأبلغ طبيبك بكل ما تتناوله لتجنّب التعارض. أوقف الدواء
واطلب رعاية عاجلة عند طفح جلدي أو تورّم بالوجه/الحلق أو صعوبة تنفّس (تحسّس محتمل).
المضادات الحيوية تعالج العدوى البكتيرية فقط وليست نزلات البرد الفيروسية.

> Medication guidance here is general. Your prescribing doctor and pharmacist are
> the authority on your specific treatment.
