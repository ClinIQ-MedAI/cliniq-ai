# ClinIQ Platform — How It Works & FAQ / كيف تعمل المنصة والأسئلة الشائعة

## What ClinIQ does
ClinIQ is a bilingual (Arabic/English) medical AI assistant. You can:
- Upload a medical scan (bone X-ray, chest X-ray, dental X-ray or photo) or a
  prescription, and get an AI-assisted, easy-to-understand explanation.
- Chat with the assistant about general health questions.
- Book an appointment with a doctor in the relevant specialty.

## How the scan analysis works
Each scan type is routed to a dedicated AI model:
- **Bone X-ray →** fracture detection.
- **Chest X-ray →** multi-disease screening with a visual heat-map (Grad-CAM).
- **Dental X-ray / photo →** decay and gum-condition screening.
- **Prescription →** handwriting-to-text extraction of drug names and doses.
Critical findings are ranked first on a triage worklist so urgent cases surface
quickly, and every AI prediction is logged with its model version for
traceability.

## Booking an appointment
Tell the assistant you want to book, choose a doctor and an available time slot,
and you will get a confirmation plus your position in that slot's queue. The AI
scan result can be attached so the doctor sees it before your visit.

## Is this a diagnosis?
No. ClinIQ is a **screening and information aid**. Every AI result must be
reviewed by a qualified clinician. The AI can miss findings or flag things that
turn out to be normal. Always follow your doctor's advice, and seek urgent care
for severe or worsening symptoms.

## Privacy
Uploads are processed to produce your result. Do not upload other people's
records. Share only what is needed for your care.

## بالعربية (ملخص)
ClinIQ مساعد طبي ذكي ثنائي اللغة. تقدر ترفع أشعة (عظام، صدر، أسنان) أو روشتة وتاخد
شرحًا مبسّطًا بمساعدة الذكاء الاصطناعي، أو تدردش في أسئلة صحية عامة، أو تحجز مع
دكتور في التخصّص المناسب. كل نوع أشعة يُوجَّه لموديل مخصّص، والحالات الحرجة تترتّب
أولًا في قائمة الفرز. **دي أداة مساعدة للفحص والتوعية وليست تشخيصًا** — لازم طبيب
مؤهّل يراجع أي نتيجة، واطلب رعاية عاجلة عند الأعراض الشديدة.
